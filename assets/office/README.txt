OFFICE BACKGROUND IMAGES
========================

Place your office photos here to display as backgrounds in the office view.

Required files (all optional):
- office-low.jpg   → Shows for office levels 0-3 (struggling phase)
- office-mid.jpg   → Shows for office levels 4-6 (getting established)
- office-high.jpg  → Shows for office levels 7-9 (corporate success)

Recommended specs:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 800x600px (or similar 4:3 aspect ratio)
- The images will be displayed with 30% opacity behind the office item list

If no images are present, the game works fine without them - you'll just see
the solid background color instead.
